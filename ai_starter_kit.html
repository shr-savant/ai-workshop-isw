Quick Prompts

Copy, paste & go
Career productivity

I am a [YOUR ROLE] at a [COMPANY TYPE]. Give me 5 specific ways I could use AI to save time in my work this week. Be concrete, not generic.

Business social media

I run a [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS] in [CITY/AREA]. Write 3 Instagram captions: a weekend special, a behind-the-scenes moment, and a community post. Each under 150 characters.

Resume rewrite

Here is one of my resume bullets: [PASTE YOUR BULLET]. Rewrite it to focus on measurable outcomes, not just duties. Give me 3 different versions.

Meeting follow-up email

Write a professional follow-up email after a meeting with [CLIENT / MANAGER]. Include key decisions, next steps, and owners. Tone: warm and direct. Max 120 words.

Advanced Prompt

Full stock analysis deep-dive

Stock Analysis Framework

Paste into Claude or ChatGPT · Replace [TICKER] with your stock symbol

Best with Claude

Act as a professional equity analyst. Analyze [TICKER SYMBOL, e.g. AAPL] using the following framework:

  • 1
    Business overview
    What does the company do, who are its customers, and what is its competitive moat?
  • 2
    Recent financials
    Revenue growth rate, gross margin trend, and free cash flow over the last 3 years.
  • 3
    Valuation
    Current P/E, forward P/E, and EV/EBITDA versus sector peers. Is the stock cheap or expensive?
  • 4
    Key risks
    Top 3 risks that could hurt the stock price over the next 12 months.
  • 5
    Bull case vs. bear case
    One paragraph for each scenario — the optimistic and the pessimistic outlook.
  • 6
    Verdict
    Buy, Hold, or Avoid — with a one-sentence rationale and a suggested entry price range.
Use publicly available data only. Be specific with numbers. Flag any figures you are uncertain about.

Core Principles

Take these home
The 85 / 15 rule
85/15

AI handles 85% — drafting, research, repetition. You provide the irreplaceable 15%: judgment, domain knowledge, and the context only you have. That's not a small thing. That's everything.

The golden rule of prompting
More context = better answers
  • ✗ "Write me an email"
  • ✓ "Write a 3-sentence follow-up to a client who hasn't replied in 2 weeks. Warm tone. Max 3 sentences."
  • ✗ "Help me with my resume"
  • ✓ "Rewrite this bullet to show outcomes, not tasks. Give me 3 versions: [paste bullet]"
  • ✗ "Analyze Apple stock"
  • ✓ "Act as an equity analyst. Analyze AAPL covering valuation, risks, bull/bear case and a buy/hold/avoid verdict."
