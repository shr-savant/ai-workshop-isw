Mobile-First Attendee Tips
📋
Create a shared prompt doc before the event. A simple Google Doc with all 3 prompts, shortened with bit.ly. Put the link on the opening slide. Mobile users tap → copy → paste. No typing under pressure.
📶
Tell attendees to use cellular data, not venue WiFi. 30 people on one network will slow things down. Say this at the start of every try-on.
🔒
Pre-warn about the account creation step. Claude free tier requires sign-up. "Hit 'Sign up with Google' — 20 seconds." Say this before Try-on #1 so nobody is surprised mid-exercise.
🤝
If someone can't get Claude working, pair them with a neighbor: "Look over their shoulder, tell them what role to type in." 2 minutes of pairing beats 10 minutes of troubleshooting.
